The Coastal Artillery Guns of Singapore

During WW2, the British built a multitude of fixed defences to protect Singapore from Japanese invasion. This webpage details for the first time since 1946 the full extent of the remaining structures, following the disorganised and rushed demolitions in 1942, hours before eminent Japanese capitulation.
